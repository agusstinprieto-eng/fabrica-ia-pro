1. Executive Dashboard

Your plant's command center. Here you visualize production pulse in real-time.

Real-Time Simulation

The numbers you see (OEE, Total Output, Cycle) are NOT static. They are directly connected to your Balancing and Costing modules. If you create a bottleneck in the line, the cycle time on the dashboard will increase and production will slow down.

OEE (Overall Equipment Effectiveness)

Measures how effective your manufacturing is. Availability x Performance x Quality.

Total Output

Count of pieces produced. This counter advances based on the actual "Cycle Time" of your balanced line.

2. AI Video Analysis

Transforms standard video into precise industrial engineering data.

  1. Upload Video: Upload an MP4 video of a manual operation (e.g., assembly, sewing).
  2. Gemini Analysis: Our AI processes frame-by-frame to identify micro-movements (Therbligs).
  3. Results: Receive a report with standard times, waste detection, and ergonomic recommendations.

Pro Tip

Ensure the operator is fully visible in the frame for better ergonomic results.

3. Line Balancing

Optimize production flow by balancing workload across stations.

Intuitive Drag & Drop

Drag operations (color blocks) from the workbench to Stations 1-4. The system automatically recalculates saturation.

  • Bottleneck: The station with the highest bar defines the speed of the ENTIRE line.
  • Efficiency: Aim for all bars to be as even as possible.
