Frequently Asked Questions

How does it integrate with my current cameras?
The system is compatible with ONVIF and RTSP protocols, allowing the use of 90% of industrial IP cameras on the market without additional hardware. It only requires a connection to the same local network or via a secure VPN.
Is my production data secure?
Absolutely. Video processing and AI can be configured to be 100% "On-Premise" (on your servers), ensuring that no sensitive data leaves your plant.
Does it require specialized personnel to operate?
No. The interface has been designed to be intuitive. Reports are generated automatically and the AI Assistant explains findings in technical natural language, acting as a mentor for your team.
What is the average ROI timeline?
Our clients report a return on investment in less than 6 months, primarily through scrap reduction and cycle time optimization detected by the AI.
