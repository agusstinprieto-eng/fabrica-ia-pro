The core optimization engine. Upload 2D cutting patterns (DXF/JSON) and let the AI calculate the most efficient distribution on wood boards. Achieves up to 96% efficiency, significantly reducing material waste.

3. Unified Hub Dashboard

Monitor your entire production line from a single screen. Key Performance Indicators (KPIs) include:

  • Throughput: Monitoring of CNC centers and Assembly lines.
